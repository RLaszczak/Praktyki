    <section class="main-section" id="IT_audit_and_hardware_audit">
      <header>
        <h2>IT audit and hardware audit</h2>
      </header>
      <p>The audit of computer equipment is usually an inventory during which they are described in detail individual components of computer sets. Inventory allows you to collect accurate data on the status of your equipment and its configuration. The audit report allows you to prepare strategies for future equipment purchases or upgrades</p>
      <hr>
      <p>Software audit most often consists in scanning computer sets and making a list of all installed ones files. Such a statement is compared with the pattern of the desired software, thanks to which it is created list of unnecessary and potentially dangerous applications. In addition, the audit report may include:</p>
      <ul>
      <li>list of the most popular applications,</li>
      <li>working time of individual programs,</li>
      <li>division of applications used for work and play,</li>
      <li>type of data stored,</li>
      <li>percentage division of disk space due to the type of stored data.</li>
    </ul>
